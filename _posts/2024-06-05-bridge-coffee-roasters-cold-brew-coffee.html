 As local as I can get!

The Brazil Single Origin 01 from Bridge Coffee Roasters, located in my hometown of Ystrad Mynach in the county of Caerphilly, caught my eye recently.

Local Coffee for Local People

Setup and The Taste

With 14 hour Cold Brewing Period in my Bodum Cold Brew Coffee Maker, This Brazilian number came out very smooth and very tasty with a nice hint of walnut.

Only a bit of Water was needed, and it can be drank black, which is always a winner.

Considerations

The price per 250g was Â£10.95, which is a bit on the expensive side. However, as of the 23rd of May, Bridge has ceased selling directly to consumers and is only now being sold wholesale.

It's a shame, as the coffee was one of the best, but not strong, coffees I've had in a long time.
